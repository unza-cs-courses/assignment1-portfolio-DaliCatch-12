About Me

I am Dalitso Zulu, a software engineering student with a strong interest in web development, user-focused design, and modern digital experiences. My journey in technology started with curiosity about how websites and applications work, and over time that curiosity grew into a passion for building projects that are both functional and visually appealing. I enjoy learning how structure, styling, and interactivity come together to create meaningful solutions on the web.

I am particularly interested in responsive web design because I believe websites should look professional and work well on all devices, from mobile phones to large desktop screens. I have been developing my skills in HTML5, CSS3, Flexbox, CSS Grid, Git, and JavaScript, and I enjoy applying these tools in portfolio projects and coursework. I also value clean code, accessibility, and organized layouts because they improve both the user experience and the quality of development work.

My goal is to continue growing as a developer, strengthen my front-end and software engineering skills, and build projects that solve real problems. What makes me unique is my willingness to keep learning, my attention to visual presentation, and my interest in combining technical development with creativity.
